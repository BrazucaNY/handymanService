Frequently Asked Handyman Questions

At Rodrigues Handyman, we aim to address the most common questions you might have regarding home maintenance, repairs, and improvement projects. Whether you’re looking for info on plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, painting, or general handyman services, we’ve got you covered.

Our experts have compiled a comprehensive resource to help you better understand and navigate the world of home repairs, ensuring your home stays safe, functional, and comfortable in New York City.

It's crucial to ensure that the handyman is both licensed and insured. This protects you from potential liabilities in case of accidents or damages during the project. Always ask to see proof of their credentials.

Handymen often have specific areas of expertise. Clarify whether they can handle the particular tasks you need assistance with, such as plumbing, electrical work, or carpentry.

Requesting references or reading online reviews can give you insight into the handyman's reliability and quality of work. A reputable professional should have positive feedback from previous clients.

Understanding the pricing structure is essential. Some handymen charge by the hour, while others have flat rates for specific tasks. Ensure you get a clear estimate before work begins to avoid unexpected costs.

Discuss the handyman's schedule to ensure they can accommodate your timeline. It's important to know when they can start and how long the project is expected to take.
