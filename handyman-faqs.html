Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our handyman services. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

Rodrigues Handyman offers a comprehensive range of home maintenance and repair services including plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, painting, drywall repair, tiling, and general handyman services. We also provide home improvement and renovation services.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

+

We understand that some repairs can't wait. For emergency services, we offer same-day or next-day service whenever possible. For non-emergency requests, we typically schedule appointments within 2-3 business days, depending on our current workload.

Are your handymen licensed and insured?

+

Yes, all our handymen are fully licensed, bonded, insured, and background-checked. We take pride in maintaining the highest professional standards to ensure your complete peace of mind.

Do you offer free estimates?

+

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all our services. Our representative will visit your property, assess the work needed, and provide you with a detailed, transparent quote.

What areas do you serve?

+

We proudly serve the entire New York City area, including all five boroughs. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, please don't hesitate to contact us with your address, and we'll be happy to confirm.

What forms of payment do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, checks, and cash for your convenience. Payment is typically due upon completion of the work unless other arrangements have been made in advance.

Do you provide a warranty on your work?

+

Yes, we stand behind all our work with a 1-year warranty on labor. If any issues arise due to our workmanship within the warranty period, we'll return to make it right at no additional cost to you.

How do I schedule a service?

+

Scheduling service is easy! You can call us at (516) 415-8918, fill out our online contact form, or email us at info@rodrigueshandyman.com. Our friendly staff will help you find a convenient appointment time that works with your schedule.
