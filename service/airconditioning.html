Air Conditioning Services

1. Clean or Replace Air Filters
    • Recommended every 1–3 months.
    • Helps eliminate dust, odor, and allergens.
    • Improves overall air circulation and system efficiency.

2. Basic Coil Cleaning
    • Clean accessible evaporator and condenser coils.
    • Helps prevent ice buildup and poor airflow.
    • Enhances cooling performance.

3. Clear Condensate Drain Lines
    • Remove clogs to prevent water leaks and mold.
    • Flush drain line using safe cleaning solutions.
    • Inspect for signs of overflow or blockage.

4. Thermostat Checks & Battery Replacement
    • Ensure proper temperature readings.
    • Replace old or dead batteries.
    • Basic programming support (if needed).

5. Air Vent & Duct Grille Cleaning
    • Remove visible dust and buildup from vents.
    • Clean grilles and covers for better airflow.
    • Helps reduce allergens and odors.

6. AC Unit General Inspection
    • Check for unusual noise, vibration, or leaks.
    • Identify minor issues before they become bigger problems.
    • Provide basic maintenance report to client.

7. Filter Supply & Installation
    • Provide and install standard-size air filters.
    • Dispose of old filters safely.
    • Optional: Monthly/quarterly filter replacement plans.

Our AC maintenance helps improve comfort, reduce dust, and prevent avoidable breakdowns — perfect for residential and light commercial spaces.
