Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

Handyman repairs, remodeling, kitchen and bathroom renovations, carpentry, painting, flooring, electrical, and plumbing services.

Do you provide estimates?

Yes, free estimates are available for all service and remodeling projects.

Are you insured?

Yes, Here Handyman is fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind.

How do you price your work?

Pricing can be based on the scope of the project — we provide clear cost breakdowns and explain whether the rate is hourly or a flat price before work begins.

Will you bring materials or should I provide them?

We can supply materials tailored to your project, or work with materials you prefer. Just let us know what you’d like when requesting your estimate.

Do I need to be home during the service visit?

You’re welcome to be present, especially for the first visit. If you can’t be home, we can discuss arrangements that work best for you ahead of time.
