## 🏠 Project-Specific Questions

### Kitchen Remodeling

### Q: How long does a kitchen remodel take?
**A:** Typical kitchen remodels take 2-4 weeks depending on:
- Scope of work (partial vs. full remodel)
- Material availability and ordering time
- Permit requirements
- Custom work complexity

### Q: Do you handle permits for kitchen remodels?
**A:** Yes, we handle all necessary permits and ensure all work meets local building codes and regulations.

### Q: Can I live in my home during a kitchen remodel?
**A:** Yes, most homeowners can remain in their homes during kitchen remodels. We minimize disruption and maintain clean work areas.

### Bathroom Renovation

### Q: How long does a bathroom renovation take?
**A:** Bathroom renovations typically take 1-3 weeks depending on:
- Scope (update vs. complete renovation)
- Tile work complexity
- Custom shower or tub installation
- Material availability

### Q: Do you waterproof bathroom areas?
**A:** Yes, we use proper waterproofing membranes and techniques for all wet areas to prevent water damage and ensure longevity.

### Q: Can you work with my existing plumbing?
**A:** We evaluate existing plumbing and recommend updates when necessary for safety, code compliance, or better functionality.

### Flooring Projects

### Q: How long does flooring installation take?
**A:** Installation time varies by material:
- **Hardwood**: 2-4 days (including finish time)
- **Tile**: 3-5 days (including grout curing)
- **Vinyl/Laminate**: 1-2 days
- **Carpet**: 1 day

### Q: Do you move furniture for flooring installation?
**A:** We can move furniture for an additional fee, or we can provide guidance on what needs to be moved before we arrive.

### Q: What about subfloor preparation?
**A:** Yes, we handle all subfloor preparation, including leveling, repair, and moisture barrier installation as needed.

### Q: What constitutes an emergency?
**A:** Emergency situations include:
