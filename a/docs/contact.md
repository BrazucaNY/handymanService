## 💰 Payment & Billing

### Payment Methods
We accept multiple payment options:
- **Cash and personal checks**
- **Credit and debit cards**
- **Electronic payment options**
- **Payment plans** for larger projects
- **Insurance claim coordination** when applicable

### Payment Schedules
Payment structures vary by project size:

#### Small Projects (Under $1,000)
- **Payment in full** upon completion
- **Walkthrough approval** before final payment

#### Medium Projects ($1,000 - $10,000)
- **50% deposit** to schedule and order materials
- **50% final payment** upon completion and approval

#### Large Projects (Over $10,000)
- **30% deposit** to begin planning and ordering
- **40% progress payment** at key milestones
